About

Bridging visual generation, communication, and machine intelligence.

I am currently a professor at the School of Information and Communication Engineering and the State Key Laboratory of Media Convergence and Communication, Communication University of China.

I received my Ph.D. from Peking University in July 2021, where I worked with Prof. Wen Gao and Prof. Siwei Ma at the Institute of Digital Media.

Before that, I earned both a B.E. in Digital Media Technology and a B.A. in Journalism from Communication University of China in 2016.

I was also a visiting Ph.D. student at the Vision and Learning Lab, University of California, Merced, under the supervision of Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang.

My research interests lie in AIGC, including image and video controllable generation and editing, as well as image and video compression based on generative models.

Students

Prospective Ph.D., M.S., and undergraduate students are welcome to apply.

I am continuously seeking highly motivated students with strong interests in controllable generation, image and video editing, generative compression, and multimedia intelligence.

  • Please send your CV, transcript when applicable, and a brief statement of research interests to qimao@cuc.edu.cn.
  • Applicants with strong coding ability, mathematical maturity, and clear research motivation are especially encouraged to get in touch.
  • Students from related areas are welcome, including computer vision, graphics, multimedia, machine learning, and digital media technology.
  • For additional team updates, please follow the group's WeChat official account: cuc-mipg.
